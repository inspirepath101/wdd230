What Is VS-Chamber of Commerce?

VS-Chamber of Commerce is a local association to promote and protect the interests of the business community in a particular place. it is a network of business minded people design to promote and protect the interests of its members. often made up of a grouup of business owners that share local interests or can also be international in scope.
